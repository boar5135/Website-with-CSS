Logistics of Soccer

The 11 Positions

  • Goalkeeper - responsible for keeping the ball out of the net and is the only one on the field permitted to use hands
  • Right back - responsible for staying connected with the other three members of the defensive line, can take space and advance up the field to get involved in the attack in the wide areas of the field
  • Left back - same responsibilities as right back
  • Center Back - responsible for shutting down any attack that progresses past the midfield; communicate to the other three defenders and make sure that every every defender is marking an offensive member of the other team
  • Center Back/Sweeper - shares communication responsibility with other center back, sometimes will play deeper than other center back as a second line of defense
  • Defending/Holding Midfielder- responsible for switching the point of attack, winning goal kicks and punts, and winning balls in the middle third of the field and quickly distributing them to the other players to attack in transition
  • Right Midfielder - responsible for receiving the ball from the center mids and/or defenders and advancing the ball up the flanks of the field as quickly as possible, crossing, and finishing from outside the box when necessary
  • Attacking Midfielder - responsible for taking players on (1vs.1) with the ball and getting the ball to the forwards in dangerous areas in front of the goal, also a primary player to look for shots on goal from distance or from inside the 18-yard box
  • Left Midfielder - same responsibilities as right midfielder
  • Right/Center Forward - responsible for scoring the majority of the team's goals and taking on players 1 vs. 1
  • Left/Center Forward - same responsibilities as right forward
